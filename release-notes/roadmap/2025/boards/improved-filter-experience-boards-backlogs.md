# Improved Filtering Experience in Boards and Backlogs

> [!NOTE]
> This feature is being released to boards and backlogs in April 2026. We plan to bring the same featuer to sprint boards and backlogs in Q2 of 2026.

We are enhancing the filtering experience in boards and backlogs, with plans to extend these improvements to queries and sprints. You will be able to filter on more fields, including custom fields. Any field shown as a column in backlogs or displayed on cards in boards can be used in filters.

This update is based on a [popular suggestion](https://developercommunity.visualstudio.com/t/add-the-ability-to-filter-boards-by-custom-fields/606538) from our user community.
