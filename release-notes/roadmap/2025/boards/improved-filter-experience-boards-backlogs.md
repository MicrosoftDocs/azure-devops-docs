# Improved filtering experience in boards and backlogs

> [!NOTE]
> This feature rolls out to boards and backlogs in April 2026. We plan to bring the same feature to sprint boards and sprint backlogs in Q2 2026.

We are improving filtering in boards and backlogs and plan to extend these improvements to queries and sprints. You will be able to filter on more fields, including custom fields. You can use any field shown as a backlog column or displayed on a board card.

This update is based on a [popular suggestion](https://developercommunity.visualstudio.com/t/add-the-ability-to-filter-boards-by-custom-fields/606538) from our user community.
