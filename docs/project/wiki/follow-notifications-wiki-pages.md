# Follow a wiki page, get notifications

[!INCLUDE [version-eq-azure-devops](../../includes/version-eq-azure-devops.md)]

Follow a Wiki page to receive email notifications whenever the page is edited, deleted, or renamed.

You receive immediate notifications for renames and deletions.
For edits, you receive a 30-minute digest email that includes all edits made within that period.
This digest helps prevent your inbox from being spammed with individual edit notifications.

## Prerequisites

[!INCLUDE [wiki-prerequisites](includes/wiki-prerequisites.md)]

## Follow a wiki page

To follow a Wiki page, select the **Follow** button on the page you want to track.
You can manage your notification settings in your user profile to ensure you receive updates as needed.

:::image type="content" source="media/wiki/follow-wiki-page.png" alt-text="Screenshot showing the Follow button on a wiki page." border="true":::

When you create a page, you become a default follower of the page.

## Unfollow a wiki page

You can unfollow a wiki page in the following ways:

- **UI:** Select the **Unfollow** button on the page.

  :::image type="content" source="media/wiki/unfollow-button.png" alt-text="Screenshot showing the Unfollow button selected on a wiki page." border="true":::

- **Footer of your email notification:** Select the **Unfollow** link in your email notification.

  :::image type="content" source="media/wiki/unfollow-wiki-page.png" alt-text="Screenshot showing the Unfollow link in a wiki email notification." border="true":::

> [!NOTE]
> When a user deletes a page, all followers are removed.
